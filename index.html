Score components — how it's calculated
Score = weighted average of 3 components. Each scored 0–10 then combined.
RS Margin weight 40%
—
Formula
margin = avg_RS_of_held_assets − 1.0
score = clip(margin ÷ 0.20 × 5 + 5, 0, 10)
margin 0.00 → score 5 · margin +0.20 → score 10 · margin −0.20 → score 0
Signal Consistency weight 35%
—
Formula
streak = consecutive months holding same assets
score = min(3 + streak × 2.33, 10)
0 months → 3.0 · 1 month → 5.3 · 2 months → 7.7 · 3+ months → 10.0
Volume Confirmation weight 25%
—
Formula
vol_ratio = 4wk avg volume ÷ 13wk avg volume
score = fraction of held assets with vol_ratio ≥ 1.0 × 10
0/2 assets confirm → 0 · 1/2 → 5.0 · 2/2 → 10.0
Composite formula
score = 0.40 × RS_margin + 0.35 × consistency + 0.25 × volume
threshold 5.5 → below = cash 100% (hidden filter) · above = follow strategy normally
